Intel Stage Cup

Organised by our hands.

It expands LAN opportunities and strengthens the competitive ecosystem for professional players and organisations. With 16 teams per regional cluster, the tournament offers a high-level environment for squads to gain experience, refine their performance, and build long-term sustainability ahead of the upcoming major. It also provides non-major-qualified teams the chance to compete in a LAN setting, accelerating their growth and exposure on the international stage.

The competition is divided into regional events: Europe, MENA, and SSA compete in the EMEA Intel Stage Cup, North and South America in the AM Intel Stage Cup, and APAC with OCE in the APAC Intel Stage Cup. This setup lets teams face opponents from outside their usual circuits, creating meaningful international matchups within a structured format. Matches will be held in Brussels, bringing the action to a central European venue.

Backed by Intel, the tournament represents a significant investment in the growth of Rocket League as an esport, offering both prestige and practical development opportunities for teams aiming to elevate their performance on the world stage.
